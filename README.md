## Superstore Sales Performance Analysis

**Tools:** Google Sheets
**Skills:** Pivot Tables, VLOOKUP, Data Cleaning, Data Visualization

## Business Problem
Analyzed 9,994 retail transactions to identify revenue and profitability 
trends across product categories and regions.

## Key Findings
- Furniture has a critically low 2.5% profit margin despite $742K in sales
- Central region underperforms with 7.9% margin vs West's 14.9%
- Labels, Paper and Envelopes are the most profitable sub-categories (42%+ margins)

Data cleaning, pivot tables, VLOOKUP, dashboard design, business insight generation
